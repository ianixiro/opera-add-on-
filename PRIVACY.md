# Discord Browser Integration - Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** June 8, 2026

## Overview
This Privacy Policy explains how Discord Browser Integration ("the Add-on") collects, uses, and protects your information.

## What Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Discord Token**: Used to authenticate with Discord API
- **Message Content**: Stored temporarily to display in the interface
- **User Preferences**: Theme, notification settings, etc.

### Information Automatically Collected
- **Usage Analytics**: Features used, frequency (anonymous)
- **Error Logs**: For troubleshooting purposes
- **Add-on Version**: To track updates

## How We Use Your Information

1. **Authentication**: Your Discord token is used solely to access your Discord account
2. **Feature Delivery**: To display servers, channels, and messages
3. **Functionality**: To enable message sending (Premium feature)
4. **Improvement**: Analyzing usage to improve features
5. **Support**: Diagnosing issues when you report them

## Data Storage

- **Local Storage**: Most data stored on your device only
- **Encrypted**: Sensitive data (token) stored in encrypted Chrome storage
- **Temporary**: Messages are not permanently stored
- **Optional Sync**: You can enable cloud sync for preferences (disabled by default)

## Data Security

✅ We protect your data through:
- Encryption in transit (HTTPS)
- Secure storage mechanisms
- No logging of message content on servers
- No sharing with third parties
- Regular security reviews

## What We Don't Do

❌ We do NOT:
- Store your Discord token on our servers
- Log or read your private messages
- Sell or share your data
- Use your data for advertising
- Track your browsing history
- Access files on your computer

## Third-Party Services

The add-on communicates directly with:
- **Discord API**: For accessing your Discord account
- **Opera Browser**: For extension functionality
- No other third parties have access to your data

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Request what data we have about you
- Delete your data and settings
- Uninstall the add-on (removes all local data)
- Contact us with privacy concerns

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Changes are effective when posted.
